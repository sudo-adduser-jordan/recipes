4 large leeks
4-5 large golden potateos
4 tbsp butter
1 4cup box chicken broth
2 teaspoon salt
1 cup heavy cream
chives to garnish
1 large pinch cayenne
optional creme de fresh

cut and wash leeks
melt butter
add leeks
add salt half
cook until soft
peel potateos
add potateos
add salt half
add broth
cook until soft
emulsify with tool or blender until smooth
add cream
add cayenne
garnish
